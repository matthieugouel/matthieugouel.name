Hey! 👋 I'm currently a Senior Systems Engineer at [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com), France, where I work on collecting, storing, and making sense of large-scale network datasets such as sFlow, BGP/BMP, and RPKI to understand network anomalies and outages.

I also run [nxthdr](https://nxthdr.dev), a French non-profit (*association loi 1901*) building open infrastructure for Internet experimentation. It operates a global network infrastructure for research and education, offering flexible worldwide active measurements from our own autonomous system and peering capabilities through multiple Internet exchanges. The whole platform is designed for rapid experimentation, education, and reproducibility, and built on three principles: open data, open source, and radical transparency.

I completed my PhD at [Sorbonne Université](https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/en) in 2023. My [doctoral work](https://theses.hal.science/tel-04164622v1/document) is centered on advancing our understanding of the internet infrastructure, achieved through the application of internet-wide high-speed measurements and extensive large-scale analysis. Broadly speaking, my research contributes to addressing challenges across diverse research domains, including network topology and performance, network security, and traffic engineering.

Previously, I held positions as a Software Engineer at the [French Ministry for the Armed Forces](https://www.defense.gouv.fr) and as a DevOps Engineer at [Société Générale](https://www.societegenerale.com/en).
